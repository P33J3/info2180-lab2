What’s in your mug?

5 reason’s why you should drink coffee.

September 20, 2023 by Peter–Jon Lawrence


There are many reasons why you should drink coffee, even some based on science. But we are going to keep things on a level and instead list my top 5 reasons why everyone should drink coffee.

  • It tastes good.
  • The aroma is the best thing to greet you in the morning.
  • There’s a roast for every mood.
  • Apart from water, with coffee you don't need another drink.
  • It tastes good when hot or cold.

No I am not going to bore you with explanations of each reason. Instead, I would love for you to have a cuppa as soon as you can. Just make sure you ask for decaf if it's your first time. If you already know what I am talking about, how about you try a new roast or flavour. Pumpkin Spiced Lattes will be there this time of year, every year. My personal favourite is a custom blend of Chickory, Cloves and Cinnamon. Sweet and spicy like my favourite Chinese dish... I digress.

You see, I’m so in love with coffee that I believe you will too and no it’s not because of the caffiene. Coffee is just one of those simple joys in life that warms the soul. While I am convinced that you're already in agreement with me and you’re ready to head to the nearest café, please bear in mind that I too would like a cup. Cheers.

The joy of making your own coffee

What makes the process so appealing

September 29, 2023 by Peter–Jon Lawrence


Like learning to ride a bicycle or tying your shoe laces, the feeling of accomplishment you get once you have done these things puts you in another realm of confidence. But these things my friend, are child’s play when compared to mastering a recipe unique to your own taste buds.

Following the basic recipe is easy. Usually a tablespoon of ground coffee, but do you prefer it medium or coarse? Then you add 6oz of water and wait 4 minutes, but do you like your flavour bold, mild or slightly bitter which seems just right when you're adding some condensed milk to it. You see? Like Goldilocks we want it just right.

Once you've found the right combination for you, you'll see how proficient you get making your cuppa while still half asleep. When you feel comfortable and if you so choose, play around with spices and blends to find that unique taste that makes you sigh with exquisite joy. Cheers.
